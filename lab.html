Cryptocurrency Analysis

AIM

Primary objective of this project is to develop a user-friendly and interactive dashboard that leverages historical data of various crypto coins. The dashboard will enable users to explore and analyze the performance of individual cryptocurrencies and the market as a whole, with the aim of providing them with valuable insights to aid in their investment decisions.
